Who are we

Welcome to Qalami, an online Quran and Arabic academy committed to providing quality education to Muslims worldwide. Our academy is designed to help students learn and memorize the Quran and Arabic language, regardless of their location, background, or level of proficiency in Arabic.

Our mission

At Qalami, our mission is to provide our students with the knowledge and skills needed to understand and memorize the Quran and Arabic language. We believe that every Muslim should have access to quality education and resources to help them deepen their understanding of Islam and the Quran.

Our Programs

We offer a range of programs that cater to different levels of proficiency in Arabic and Quran memorization. Our comprehensive Quran memorization course starts with the basics of the Arabic language, including the alphabet, pronunciation, and grammar. Students progress through the course, memorizing verses of the Quran in chronological order, starting with the shorter chapters and gradually moving on to longer ones.
We also offer an intensive Quran memorization course for students who have some prior knowledge of Arabic. This course is designed to help students memorize the entire Quran within a specific timeframe, usually two to three years. The course includes regular assessments to track progress and ensure that students are on track to meet their goals.
In addition to Quran memorization courses, we also offer courses in Islamic studies and Arabic language. These courses are designed to help students deepen their understanding of Islam and the Arabic language, making it easier for them to memorize the Quran and comprehend its meaning.

Our Instructors

Our instructors are highly qualified and experienced in teaching Quran and Islamic studies. They use modern teaching methods and technology to create a dynamic and interactive learning environment. Students can interact with their teachers and peers, ask questions, and receive feedback on their progress.

Flexible Learning Options

At Qalami, we understand that students have busy schedules and may need to balance their academic, professional, and personal commitments while pursuing their Quran memorization goals. That's why we offer flexible learning options, so students can learn at their own pace, from the comfort of their homes.
We use advanced technology to ensure that our students have access to high-quality resources and materials, regardless of their location. We also provide regular assessments and progress reports to help our students stay on track and achieve their goals.
