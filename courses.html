Introduction to our courses

Welcome to our Quran and Arabic courses, where we are committed to providing quality education to Muslims worldwide. Our courses are designed to help students learn and memorize the Quran and Arabic language, regardless of their location, background, or level of proficiency in Arabic.

The Islamic part in our courses

At Qalami, we believe that Islamic knowledge is essential to a complete education, which is why we integrate the teachings of Islam into all of our courses to provide our students with a comprehensive understanding of the Quran and the Islamic faith.

Comprehensive Quran Memorization Course

Our comprehensive Quran memorization course is designed to help beginners start their journey towards memorizing the Quran. The course starts with the basics of the Arabic language, including the alphabet, pronunciation, and grammar. Students progress through the course, memorizing verses of the Quran in chronological order, starting with the shorter chapters and gradually moving on to longer ones.

Intensive Quran Memorization Course

Our intensive Quran memorization course is designed for students who have some prior knowledge of Arabic. This course is designed to help students memorize the entire Quran within a specific timeframe, usually two to three years. The course includes regular assessments to track progress and ensure that students are on track to meet their goals.

Arabic Language Courses

We offer a range of Arabic language courses to help students deepen their understanding of the language. Our courses are designed to help students learn the Arabic language from scratch, starting with the basics and gradually progressing to more advanced concepts. We also offer courses that cater to different levels of proficiency in Arabic, so students can choose the course that best suits their needs.

Islamic Studies Courses

We offer a range of Islamic studies courses to help students deepen their understanding of Islam. Our courses cover a wide range of topics, including the Quran, Hadith, Fiqh, and Tafsir. Our courses are designed to help students understand the principles and concepts of Islam, so they can apply them in their daily lives.
